About Us

We strive to provide valuable content and services to our users but please let us know if you spot something we could improve!

Ferret Facts is based in Louisville, Kentucky and originally started as a school project for a System Design class. Ferret Facts seeks to inspire current and future ferret lovers to learn and talk about what they love most, Ferrets!
